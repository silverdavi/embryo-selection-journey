METHODOLOGICAL INTEGRITY

Unlike models claiming AUC >0.9 (which use non-transferred embryos as negatives or human scores as ground truth), Bonna validates exclusively against Known Implantation Data (KID) — fetal heartbeat confirmed at 5-8 weeks post-transfer. This is the hardest, most clinically honest benchmark.
