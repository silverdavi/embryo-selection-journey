ON AUCS ABOVE 0.9 IN THIS FIELD

Several published embryo-AI papers report AUC > 0.9. Those numbers usually reflect benchmarks against embryologist scores (not KID), inclusion of non-transferred embryos as negatives, or pooled Day-3 and Day-5 cohorts. Bonna is reported only against KID with fetal heartbeat at 5–8 weeks post-transfer. On that benchmark, 0.754 is the highest we are aware of in peer-reviewed literature.
