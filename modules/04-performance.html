WHY THIS AUC NUMBER IS HONEST

Several embryo-AI papers report AUC > 0.9. Read the methods. Those numbers typically come from (a) benchmarking against embryologist scores rather than Known Implantation Data, (b) including non-transferred embryos as negatives, or (c) mixing Day 3 and Day 5 cohorts. Bonna reports only AUC against KID with fetal heartbeat confirmed at 5–8 weeks post-transfer — the hardest, most clinically meaningful benchmark. An AUC of 0.754 on this benchmark is currently the best published.
