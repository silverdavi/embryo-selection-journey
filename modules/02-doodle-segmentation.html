STAGE 02 · COMPUTER VISION PREPROCESSING

DoodleP2P: segmenting the zona, cavity, and ICM+TE before scoring

DoodleP2P is a Pix2Pix GAN trained on 3,217 hand-annotated embryo frames. It converts a raw time-lapse image into a three-channel mask: zona pellucida, blastocoel cavity, and trophectoderm + ICM. The mask discards brightness, focal depth, and optics, which is why Bonna runs unmodified on EmbryoScope, Geri, and Miri.

Case · Preprocessing step Case #47 · Day 5 Blastocyst EmbryoScope™ + · Frame 184 · 113h post-insemination
STAGE 02

Case #47’s frame 184 (full expansion, pre-hatching) enters DoodleP2P as a 500×500 px grayscale image. The GAN emits a three-channel RGB mask: outer ring = zona pellucida, central fluid = blastocoel cavity, dense region = trophectoderm + ICM. The same frame would be imaged very differently on the clinic’s Miri™ TL (Esco) or Geri™ (Genea Biomedx), but the segmentation output is normalized — which is why Bonna is validated across four countries without clinic-specific retraining.

Pipeline · How DoodleP2P is trained and deployed
  1. 01
    Manual annotation. 3,217 embryo frames across developmental stages (morula through hatched blastocyst) were hand-segmented by trained embryologists to produce ground-truth masks.
  2. 02
    Custom Pix2Pix GAN (DoodleP2P). A conditional GAN learns the pixel-wise map from raw microscopy to three-class segmentation. The generator is a U-Net with residual blocks; the discriminator is a PatchGAN.
  3. 03
    Optical normalization. The pseudo-colored mask discards brightness, light source, focal depth, and magnification artifacts — enabling cross-vendor deployment without per-clinic recalibration.
  4. 04
    Feature extraction. A dedicated DoodleEncoder downstream of the mask extracts geometric features (zona thickness, cavity expansion ratio, ICM area) that feed the Bonna ensemble (Stage 03).
CHANNEL 1
Zona pellucida. Thickness correlates with hatching potential; thinning cues imminent hatching.
CHANNEL 2
Blastocoel cavity. Expansion ratio maps to Gardner stage (3/4/5/6).
CHANNEL 3
ICM + trophectoderm. Morphology maps to Gardner A/B/C grading.

HARDWARE PORTABILITY

Bonna was trained in Israel and has since been validated on clinic data from Spain, Ukraine, and France without per-site retraining. The portability comes from DoodleP2P: once the frame is reduced to a 3-channel biological mask, the downstream network sees the same input regardless of camera, light source, or magnification.
