INFERENCE-TIME AUGMENTATION

Each image undergoes 16 random augmentations × 16 dropout simulations = 256 forward passes per embryo. This Monte Carlo approach produces a score distribution whose standard deviation quantifies prediction uncertainty — enabling clinically meaningful confidence reporting.
