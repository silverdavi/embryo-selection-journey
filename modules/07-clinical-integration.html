Embryologist + Algorithm

Bonna integrates into GenPrime’s clinical workflow as a decision-support tool — complementing embryologist expertise with objective, calibrated predictions.

RUNNING EXAMPLE Embryo #47 — Day 5 Blastocyst Patient: 34yo, AMH 2.1 ng/mL, BMI 24.2

Embryo #47 appears on the clinic dashboard ranked #2 of 7 in the cycle, with p_implantation = 72% and a high confidence badge. The embryologist reviews the score alongside their own Gardner BB assessment. They select #47 for transfer, supported by Bonna's objective analysis. Result: positive fetal heartbeat at 7 weeks, confirmed ongoing pregnancy at 12 weeks. The AI didn't replace the embryologist — it gave them the confidence to trust their instinct on a borderline call.

THE CLINICAL WORKFLOW
PRE-TREATMENT

Calculator generates personalized cycle predictions from age, AMH, BMI, and health factors. Multi-cycle cumulative probabilities support informed consent.

DURING TREATMENT

Bonna provides objective implantation scores for each embryo alongside embryologist assessment. Confidence scores flag when to trust vs investigate further.

POST-ANALYSIS

Cycle-level live birth probability computed across all embryos. Results delivered via real-time WebSocket to the clinic dashboard.

THE PHILOSOPHY

Bonna is not a replacement for the embryologist. It’s a calibrated second opinion that never gets tired, never suffers from value normalization bias, and processes each embryo independently of the others in the cohort. When human expertise meets algorithmic objectivity, patients get the best of both.
