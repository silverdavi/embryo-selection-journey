A NOTE ON WHERE AI HELPS MOST

AI’s marginal value is not uniform across your casebook. It is largest in the fair-quality stratum where embryologist agreement is lowest, and smallest in clearly poor (CC, arrest) and clearly good (AA) cases where kappa is already high. In the fair stratum we see a roughly 14-point AUC lift over the embryologist-panel mean. That is where your transfer decisions change, and where the patient benefit accrues.
