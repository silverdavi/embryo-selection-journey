WHERE THE LIFT IS

On clearly poor (CC, arrest) and clearly good (AA) blastocysts, panel κ is already 0.65 and AI adds little. On the fair-quality stratum — which is where most fresh-transfer decisions actually live — κ is 0.25 and Bonna’s AUC is about 14 points above the 36-panel mean. That is the stratum where the model changes outcomes.
